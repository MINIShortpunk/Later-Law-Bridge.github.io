Welcome to Later Law Bridge

A community for mature learners (30+) studying law or exploring a legal career. No matter where you started — we're here to help you bridge the gap.

What We Offer

  • Interviews: Real conversations with legal professionals who took the long way in
  • Podcast: The Long Way In — for mature learners, by a mature learner
  • Case Explainers: Videos breaking down the law in plain English
  • Community: Study support, co-working sessions, and real connections
