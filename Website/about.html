About Later Law Bridge

Our Mission

To make a career in law genuinely reachable for adults who came to it later — by sharing the conversations, study support and role models that turn the long way in from an isolating climb into a connected journey.

Who We're For

Mature learners aged 30+ who are studying law, planning to study, or considering it. Whether you're returning to an LLB, exploring the SQE route, or just curious — this is a space built for you.

Founded By

Maria O'Sullivan — a mature LLB student at Arden University who came to law the long way. Every decision on Later Law Bridge is guided by lived experience, not assumption.

Our Values

  • Lived experience is currency. Your story matters.
  • Warm but rigorous. Professional standards, human tone.
  • Joyful. Mature doesn't mean tired.
  • Inclusive by design. Always free at the point of access.
  • Honest. We show our working.
